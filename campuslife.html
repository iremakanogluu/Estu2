Nutrition

Meals are served at our University's 2 Eylül Campus and Porsuk Campus in order to ensure that our students have a healthy and high-quality diet. Also canteen, cafeteria, dining halls etc. Social facilities also serve our students. Also canteen, cafeteria, dining halls etc. Social facilities also serve our students.

Transportation

Transportation to our campuses is provided by buses and minibuses departing from different points of the city. Students meet their accommodation needs with KYK dormitories, private dormitories and apartments located in different grades of the city. There is also a KYK dormitory near the campus.

Library

With its strong electronic resource collection, 130 000 e-books and 30 000 printed book collections, students and academicians are physically served in our central library and reading room.


Sports Facilities

In our university's sports halls, stadium and qualified conference halls with the capacity to organize international olympics and competitions; It hosts congresses and symposiums and offers students a unique campus atmosphere both scientifically and socially.

Student Clubs

Student clubs of our university are waiting for you in order to develop individual skills, gain the ability to work in teams, and meet the social and cultural needs of our students.Our university supports our students in joining the clubs they prefer or establishing their own clubs.

Health Service

Both our students and staff can benefit from the Education Family Health Center of our university. Currently, two outpatient clinics are actively serving in the center, providing examination and prescription writing, injection and dressing, and blood tests.
