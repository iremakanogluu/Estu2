Eskişehir Technical University Home Page

About Our University Established with the Law No. 7141 published in the Official Gazette dated May 18, 2018 and numbered 30425, Eskişehir Technical University has 5 Faculties, 2 Vocational Schools and 3 Institutes and 4 Institutes in 2 campuses located in the center of Eskişehir, which is a city of science, culture and also a youth city. It consists of a Research and Application Center.

Education

With the vision of "Being a pioneer of change and transformation by creating value", we contribute to the academic, personal, cultural and social development of students at the highest level and support them to enter their business life as qualified individuals who are proud and honored to be a member of Eskişehir Technical University, and who can take a place in global organizations and environments.

Research

Research and Graduate Processes Directorate (ALSD), in line with the mission, vision, core values and policies of Eskişehir Technical University, ensures the development of talents and skills in the field of research in harmony, in terms of quantity and quality, which will enable the University to rank at the top in national and international environments.

Airport

The opening of Eskişehir Hasan Polatkan Airport to traffic is based on the establishment of the Civil Aviation Vocational School (SHMYO), which forms the foundations of Eskişehir Technical University Faculty of Aviation and Space Sciences, in 1986.
